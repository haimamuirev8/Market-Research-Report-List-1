<p><p>The hydraulic actuator market is categorized mainly into linear and rotary actuators. Linear actuators convert hydraulic energy into straight-line motion, commonly used in applications like industrial machinery and automation systems. In contrast, rotary actuators produce rotational movement, ideal for tasks requiring torque and angular movement, such as valve control and robotic arms. Both types leverage hydraulic power for high efficiency, precise control, and robustness, making them essential in various industries, including manufacturing, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
