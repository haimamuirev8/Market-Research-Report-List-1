<p><p>The soft tape measure market encompasses various types, primarily pocket tapes and surveyor tapes. Pocket tapes are compact, flexible measuring tools designed for easy portability, commonly used in sewing and craft applications. They typically feature both metric and imperial measurements for versatility. Surveyor tapes, on the other hand, are longer and sturdier, intended for land measurement and construction projects. These tapes are often marked in large increments to facilitate accurate surveying over greater distances, meeting the specific needs of professionals in the field.</p></p>
