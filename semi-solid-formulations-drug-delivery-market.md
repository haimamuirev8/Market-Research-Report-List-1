<p><p>The semi-solid formulations drug delivery market encompasses various dosage forms, including creams, ointments, lotions, gels, and pastes. Creams are emulsions that hydrate and protect the skin, while ointments are thicker, offering enhanced occlusiveness for dry skin treatment. Lotions are fluid emulsions suitable for larger areas, facilitating easy application. Gels provide a cooling effect and quick absorption with a non-greasy texture. Pastes combine high viscosity and occlusiveness, often used for localized treatments, making these formulations versatile for topical applications.</p></p>
