<p><p>The sanitary flange check valve market comprises three primary types: welding type, thread type, and flange type. Welding type valves are permanently joined to pipes, ensuring a leak-proof seal, making them ideal for high-pressure systems. Thread type valves utilize threaded connections for easy installation and maintenance, suitable for applications needing frequent part replacements. Flange type valves feature a flat surface with bolt holes for secure attachment to piping systems, providing a reliable solution for various sanitary applications in food, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology.</p></p>
