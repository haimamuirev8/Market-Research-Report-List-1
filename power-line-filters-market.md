<p><ul><li>Differential Mode Interference Current</li><li>Common Mode Interference Current</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Line Filters market is categorized based on two primary types of interference: differential mode and common mode. Differential mode interference occurs between lines, affecting the signal integrity of powered devices and typically involves noise between the live and neutral wires. Common mode interference, on the other hand, involves noise that occurs simultaneously on both the live and neutral lines relative to the ground, impacting overall system performance. Both types are critical for maintaining clean power and enhancing electronic equipment reliability.</p></p>
