<p><p>Dedusters are essential in processing industries for removing unwanted particles from bulk materials. The Spiral Path Deduster utilizes a helical movement to efficiently separate contaminants by allowing continuous material flow, improving filtration efficiency. In contrast, the Elevating Path Deduster employs an upward movement, utilizing gravity to aid in dust removal while minimizing product loss. Both types cater to specific operational needs, enhancing product purity and process efficiency in various applications, such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and plastics manufacturing.</p></p>
