<p><p>The Trifloxystrobin market is categorized into three primary formulation types: EC (Emulsifiable Concentrate), SC (Suspension Concentrate), and WG (Water-Dispersible Granule). </p><p>EC formulations are liquid concentrates that can be easily mixed with water, suitable for foliar applications. SC formulations offer a stable suspension that provides effective coverage and ease of handling. WG formulations are granules that dissolve easily in water, allowing for controlled release and application in various agricultural settings. Each formulation type caters to specific crop protection needs and usage preferences.</p></p>
