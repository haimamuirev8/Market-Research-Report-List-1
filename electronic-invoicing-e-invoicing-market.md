<p><p>Electronic invoicing (e-invoicing) automates the billing process by enabling businesses to send, receive, and manage invoices electronically. The market is divided into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based e-invoicing is hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, accessibility, and reduced IT maintenance costs, making it attractive for small to medium enterprises. On-premises e-invoicing, however, is installed and run on local servers, providing enhanced security and control, making it preferable for larger organizations with specific compliance needs.</p></p>
