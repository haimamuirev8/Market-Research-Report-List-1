<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Exhausts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)</li><li>Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF)</li><li>Lean Nox Trap (LNT)</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive exhaust market includes various technologies aimed at reducing emissions from diesel engines. Diesel Oxidation Catalysts (DOC) convert harmful gases into harmless substances, while Diesel Particulate Filters (DPF) capture and store soot particles. Lean NOx Traps (LNT) reduce nitrogen oxides during lean burn conditions by trapping and releasing them under rich conditions. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) uses a urea-based solution to convert NOx emissions into nitrogen and water, enhancing overall vehicle efficiency and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
