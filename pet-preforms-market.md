Non-crystallized neck finish preforms are primarily used for standard applications, offering lower production costs and ease of processing. In contrast, crystallized neck finish preforms are designed for applications requiring higher thermal resistance and durability, making them suitable for carbonated beverages and hot-filling processes. These distinct properties cater to various packaging needs and performance requirements in the market.</p></p>
