<p><ul><li>Mechanical Diaphragm Type</li><li>Hydraulic Diaphragm Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diaphragm type metering pumps are essential in precise fluid handling across various industries. The mechanical diaphragm type uses a mechanical mechanism to create pressure differentials, allowing accurate flow control, ideal for low to medium viscosity fluids. In contrast, the hydraulic diaphragm type employs hydraulic pressure to drive the diaphragm, enabling it to handle more viscous fluids and higher pressure applications. Both types ensure leak-free operation and minimal contamination, providing reliable solutions for metering and dosing applications.</p></p>
