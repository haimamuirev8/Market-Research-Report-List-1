<p><p>The Visual Inspection Equipment market encompasses two key types: Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) and Remote Visual Inspection (RVI). NDT involves methods that assess the integrity of materials or components without causing damage, commonly used in industries like aerospace and construction. RVI, on the other hand, utilizes advanced technologies such as cameras and probes to inspect hard-to-reach areas, providing real-time visual data while minimizing disruption. Both types enhance safety, quality control, and maintenance processes across various sectors.</p></p>
