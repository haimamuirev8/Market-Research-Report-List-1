<p><ul><li>Normal Metallocene Catalyst</li><li>Dinuclear Metallocene Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallocene catalyst market is primarily divided into two types: normal metallocene catalysts and dinuclear metallocene catalysts. Normal metallocene catalysts consist of single metallocene compounds which effectively facilitate polymerization, producing high-performance polymers like polyethylene. Dinuclear metallocene catalysts, on the other hand, contain two metallocene units that enhance catalytic activity and control over polymer structure, leading to tailored properties and improved material performance. Both types are crucial for various applications in the plastics and petrochemical industries.</p></p>
