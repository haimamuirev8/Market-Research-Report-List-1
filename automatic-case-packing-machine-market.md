<p><strong>The Automatic Case Packing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>V-FFS</li><li>H-FFS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Case Packing Machine market can be divided into two primary types: Vertical Form Fill Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form Fill Seal (H-FFS). V-FFS machines operate in a vertical orientation, forming bags from flat film and filling them; they are ideal for powdery and granulated products. In contrast, H-FFS machines work horizontally, forming package containers and filling them, catering to products like bottles or cartons. Both types enhance efficiency, reduce labor costs, and improve packaging speed in various industries.</p></p>
