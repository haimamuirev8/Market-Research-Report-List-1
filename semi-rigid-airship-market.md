<p><p>The semi-rigid airship market is categorized primarily into two types: helium airships and gas airships. Helium airships utilize helium as a lifting gas, offering advantages such as safety and non-flammability, making them suitable for various applications like tourism and surveillance. In contrast, gas airships typically use lighter-than-air gases like hydrogen, which, while providing greater lift, carry higher risks due to flammability. Both types serve niche markets, balancing safety, cost, and operational efficiency for diverse aerial applications.</p></p>
