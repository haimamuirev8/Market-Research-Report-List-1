<p><p>Steering compasses are vital navigation tools used in marine and aviation applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: magnetic and electronic. Magnetic compasses operate on Earth’s magnetic field principles, providing reliable navigation but can be susceptible to errors from nearby magnetic interference. In contrast, electronic compasses use sensor technology to determine direction, offering higher accuracy and additional features like integration with GPS systems. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences within navigation environments.</p></p>
