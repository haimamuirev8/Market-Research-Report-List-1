<p><p>The nuclear valves market encompasses various types designed for safety and efficiency in nuclear applications. Gate valves are used for on/off control, providing minimal flow resistance. Globe valves offer precise flow regulation. Butterfly valves are compact and quick-acting, ideal for large pipes. Ball valves provide reliable sealing and fast operation, while check valves prevent backflow. Diaphragm valves ensure leak-free operation and are often used in corrosive environments. Other valves may include specialized designs tailored to unique nuclear system requirements.</p></p>
