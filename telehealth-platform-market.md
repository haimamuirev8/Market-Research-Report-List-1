<p><p>The telehealth platform market is typically divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based telehealth platforms operate on remote servers, offering scalability, accessibility, and reduced infrastructure costs. They enable healthcare providers to deliver services from anywhere with an internet connection. In contrast, on-premises solutions are installed locally within a healthcare facility's servers, providing greater control over data and security but requiring higher upfront investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves distinct needs in healthcare delivery.</p></p>
