<p><strong>The Milk Testing Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Milk Testing Devices</li><li>Infrared Milk Testing Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The milk testing devices market comprises primarily two types: ultrasonic and infrared milk testing devices. Ultrasonic milk testing devices utilize high-frequency sound waves to assess the quality and composition of milk by measuring properties such as fat content and solids. In contrast, infrared milk testing devices use infrared light to analyze the milk's chemical composition, offering rapid and accurate results for fat, protein, and lactose content. Both technologies are essential in quality control for dairy producers and processors.</p></p>
