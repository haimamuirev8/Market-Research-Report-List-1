<p><p>The Enterprise Thin Clients market is divided into two main types: Traditional Thin Clients and Cloud-based Thin Clients. Traditional Thin Clients are hardware devices that connect to a centralized server, offering a streamlined experience for terminal-focused applications. They rely on local processing power and typically provide improved security. In contrast, Cloud-based Thin Clients leverage virtual desktop infrastructure (VDI) and remote applications hosted in the cloud, allowing for greater flexibility, scalability, and access from various devices over the internet.</p></p>
