The market for these assays is typically divided into two main segments: plasma and serum. Plasma assays involve testing the liquid component of blood following the removal of cells, while serum assays use the liquid left after blood has clotted. Both types are crucial in evaluating coagulation disorders and monitoring therapies related to thrombosis and hemostasis.</p></p>
