<p><p>Cross-linked Polyethylene Foam (XLPE) is a versatile material known for its durability and insulation properties, used in various industries. The market is divided into two main types: physically cross-linked polyethylene foam, which is created through heat and pressure, resulting in improved strength and flexibility; and chemically cross-linked polyethylene foam, produced using chemical agents that enhance its properties and resistance to environmental factors. Both types serve applications in packaging, automotive, construction, and athletic equipment, among others.</p></p>
