<p><p>The photoelectric smoke sensor market can be categorized into single beam, double beam, and multi-beam types based on the configuration of light beams used for detecting smoke. Single beam sensors utilize one beam of light and a photodetector, making them straightforward and cost-effective. Double beam sensors employ two beams, enhancing sensitivity and reducing false alarms. Multi-beam sensors incorporate multiple beams, offering higher accuracy and increased reliability in diverse environments, effectively detecting smoke particles while minimizing interference from other elements.</p></p>
