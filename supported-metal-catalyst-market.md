<p><p>The supported metal catalyst market consists of two primary types: single metal catalysts and multimetallic catalysts. Single metal catalysts utilize a single metal, such as platinum or palladium, to facilitate chemical reactions, providing high specificity and activity. In contrast, multimetallic catalysts involve a combination of different metals, enhancing catalytic properties and allowing for a broader range of reactions. The choice between these catalysts depends on the specific application, required reaction efficiency, and desired product selectivity in various industrial processes.</p></p>
