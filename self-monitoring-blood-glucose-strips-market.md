<p><p>The self-monitoring blood glucose strips market is categorized into three main types: glucose oxidase, glucose dehydrogenase, and others. Glucose oxidase strips use the enzyme glucose oxidase to measure blood glucose levels, providing accurate and reliable results. Glucose dehydrogenase strips utilize glucose dehydrogenase enzymes, offering benefits like reduced interference from other substances. The "Other" category includes alternative technologies and innovative methods for monitoring glucose, such as electrochemical or colorimetric techniques, catering to diverse consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
