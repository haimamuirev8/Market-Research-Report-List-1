<p><p>The gamma rays collimators market comprises two main types: panoramic collimators and directional collimators. Panoramic collimators are designed to radiate gamma rays over a wide area, ensuring broad coverage for imaging or therapy applications. In contrast, directional collimators focus gamma rays in a specific direction, enhancing resolution and minimizing scatter for precise targeting in medical diagnostics and treatments. Both types are essential in nuclear medicine and radiography, optimizing performance based on application requirements.</p></p>
