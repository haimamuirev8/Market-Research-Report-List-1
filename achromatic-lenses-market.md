<p><ul><li>Positive Achromatic Lenses</li><li>Negative Achromatic Lenses</li><li>Cylindrical Achromatic Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The achromatic lenses market is categorized into three main types: positive achromatic lenses, negative achromatic lenses, and cylindrical achromatic lenses. Positive achromatic lenses focus light and correct chromatic aberrations, commonly used in microscopes and cameras. Negative achromatic lenses diverge light and are essential in optical systems to reduce distortion. Cylindrical achromatic lenses correct astigmatism by focusing light in one direction, typically utilized in specialized applications like laser systems and barcode scanners, enhancing optical performance across various industries.</p></p>
