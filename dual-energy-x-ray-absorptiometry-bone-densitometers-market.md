<p><p>The Dual Energy X-ray Absorptiometry (DXA) bone densitometer market is divided into two main types: axial and peripheral bone densitometers. Axial bone densitometers are designed for full-body scans, primarily assessing the lumbar spine and hip, offering comprehensive bone density evaluation. In contrast, peripheral bone densitometers focus on measuring bone density in peripheral sites such as the wrist or heel, providing quick and accessible assessments. Both types serve crucial roles in diagnosing and managing osteoporosis and other bone-related conditions.</p></p>
