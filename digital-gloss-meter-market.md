<p><strong>The Digital Gloss Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-angle</li><li>Dual Angles</li><li>Multi-angles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital gloss meter market consists of three primary types based on measurement angles: single-angle, dual-angle, and multi-angle gloss meters. Single-angle gloss meters measure gloss at a single specified angle, ideal for consistent surfaces. Dual-angle gloss meters assess gloss at two different angles, providing a broader view of surface characteristics. Multi-angle gloss meters measure gloss at multiple angles, capturing detailed variations in surface finish, making them suitable for a wider range of applications and material types.</p></p>
