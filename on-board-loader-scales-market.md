<p><ul><li>Static</li><li>Dynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>On-board loader scales are categorized into static and dynamic markets based on their operational methodologies. Static scales measure weight when the loader is stationary, ensuring precise readings during loading or unloading processes. In contrast, dynamic scales provide real-time weight measurement while the loader is in motion, allowing for continuous operation and efficiency. Both markets cater to distinct operational needs in various industries, with static scales focusing on accuracy and dynamic scales emphasizing productivity and time-saving in material handling.</p></p>
