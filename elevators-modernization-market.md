<p><p>The elevators modernization market is primarily divided into two types: hydraulic and traction elevators. Hydraulic elevators utilize a fluid-driven system for lifting, making them ideal for low-rise buildings due to their cost-effectiveness and smooth operation. In contrast, traction elevators rely on a pulley system and are suited for mid to high-rise structures, offering faster speeds and greater energy efficiency. Modernization of both types involves upgrading components to enhance performance, safety, and energy consumption, catering to evolving building requirements.</p></p>
