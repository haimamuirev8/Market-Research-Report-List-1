<p><p>The anticoagulant reversal drugs market includes various types aimed at counteracting the effects of anticoagulants. Protamine is utilized to neutralize heparin, while Tranexamic Acid is an antifibrinolytic that reduces bleeding. Idarucizumab specifically reverses the effects of dabigatran, and other agents may include emerging therapies or adjunctive treatments to enhance hemostasis.</p></p>
