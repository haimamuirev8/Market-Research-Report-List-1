<p><strong>The Dry Cooling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Cooled Condensers (ACCs)</li><li>Air Cooled Heat Exchangers (ACHEs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry cooling systems encompass technologies like Air Cooled Condensers (ACCs) and Air Cooled Heat Exchangers (ACHEs). ACCs utilize ambient air to condense refrigerants from vapor to liquid, ideal for power plants and industrial processes, promoting efficiency while minimizing water usage. ACHEs, on the other hand, transfer heat from fluids to air, effectively cooling various applications, including HVAC systems and chemical processes. Both systems are increasingly favored for their environmental benefits and compliance with water conservation regulations.</p></p>
