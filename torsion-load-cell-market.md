<p><ul><li>Non-Contact Torsion Load Cell</li><li>Strain Gauge Torsion Load Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The torsion load cell market comprises two primary types: non-contact torsion load cells and strain gauge torsion load cells. Non-contact torsion load cells utilize advanced technologies like optical sensors to measure torque without direct physical interaction, offering high precision and durability. In contrast, strain gauge torsion load cells employ resistive strain gauges attached to a torsion element, deforming under load to produce an electrical signal. Both types serve diverse applications in industries requiring accurate torque measurements for quality control and monitoring systems.</p></p>
