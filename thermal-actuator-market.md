<p><strong>The Thermal Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normally Closed Type</li><li>Normally Open Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal actuator market is primarily categorized into two types: normally closed and normally open. Normally closed thermal actuators maintain the closed position until heated, ideal for applications requiring a default secure state, such as in safety systems. In contrast, normally open thermal actuators start in the open position and close when heated, suitable for applications needing immediate operation, like in heating systems. Both types provide efficient temperature control for diverse industrial and residential applications, enhancing system performance and reliability.</p></p>
