<p><p>Traffic counters are classified into several types based on their detection technology. IR Beam counters utilize infrared beams to detect vehicle passage. Thermal Imaging counters detect heat emitted by vehicles, providing accurate counts even in various weather conditions. Video-Based counters use cameras and advanced algorithms to monitor and analyze traffic patterns visually. Other types may include magnetic sensors and acoustic sensors, each offering unique advantages in traffic monitoring and data collection for transportation analysis and urban planning.</p></p>
