<p><p>The rhinoscope devices market is primarily divided into two main types: video rhinoscopes and fiber rhinoscopes. Video rhinoscopes utilize advanced imaging technology to provide high-resolution visuals of the nasal cavities, allowing for enhanced diagnosis and treatment monitoring. Conversely, fiber rhinoscopes utilize flexible optical fibers to illuminate and visualize the nasal passages, offering a less invasive option for examinations. Both types are pivotal in providing accurate and efficient ENT diagnostics, facilitating improved patient outcomes in nasal health management.</p></p>
