<p><ul><li>Gravity Induced</li><li>Vibration Induced</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Outdoor Stride Sensor market is categorized into two primary types: gravity-induced and vibration-induced sensors. Gravity-induced sensors measure changes in acceleration and orientation while detecting the shifts in gravitational force as a user moves, enhancing performance tracking. Vibration-induced sensors, on the other hand, capture the vibrations generated by footfalls, analyzing these patterns to assess stride effectiveness and overall gait. Both types contribute to improved biomechanics and athletic performance monitoring in various outdoor activities.</p></p>
