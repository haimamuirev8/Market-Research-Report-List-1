<p><p>The oil-fired furnace market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Internal Condensing Oil Fired Furnaces** utilize an integrated condensing design that maximizes heat recovery, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions.</p><p>2. **External Condensing Oil Fired Furnaces** feature a separate condensing unit, allowing for flexible installation and efficient operation while also improving energy use. </p><p>3. **Non-Condensing Oil Fired Furnaces** operate without a condensate recovery system, offering simpler installation but lower efficiency compared to condensing models. Each type caters to different efficiency needs and installation preferences.</p></p>
